# Disclaimer

Clean Slate AdShield is provided for educational, research, and personal privacy use only.

## No guarantee

This project does not guarantee that every advertisement, tracker, sponsored placement, or analytics request will be blocked. Ad delivery changes frequently, and many platforms serve advertisements from the same domains used for normal content delivery.

## YouTube and streaming services

YouTube and other streaming services may deliver ads, recommendations, telemetry, and video content from shared infrastructure. DNS-level filtering may reduce some ad-related and tracking requests, but it cannot reliably remove every in-stream ad without risking broken playback or violating platform rules. This project does not bypass subscriptions, paywalls, DRM, app protections, or terms-enforcement systems.

## User responsibility

Users are responsible for complying with the terms of service of the apps, websites, networks, and platforms they use. The maintainers are not responsible for misuse, account restrictions, broken app behavior, data loss, or network issues caused by custom blocklists.

## Privacy

The starter implementation is designed to filter DNS traffic locally on the device. It should not collect browsing history or sell user data. Review the source code and privacy policy before using any modified build.
